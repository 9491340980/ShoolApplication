{{ appName }} is a school management application provided to schools. This policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and how we protect it. We keep it simple and plain.

1. Who controls the data

Each school owns the data it enters. {{ appName }} stores and processes that data on behalf of the school solely to provide the service. We do not own, sell, rent, or trade school or student data.

2. What we collect

Only what a school enters to run its operations, such as:

  • Student details (name, roll/admission number, class, parents' names, contact, and optional records like date of birth, address)
  • Attendance, marks, fees, timetables, and notices
  • Login accounts for staff and parents (name, email/phone)

3. How we use it

Strictly to operate the app for the school — recording attendance and marks, managing fees, sending the school's own notices/alerts to its parents, and generating the school's reports and report cards. We do not use it for advertising.

4. Data separation & access

Every school's data is isolated — one school can never see another school's data. Within a school, access is limited by role (Head Master, Teacher, Parent, Student). Our team accesses a school's data only when needed to provide support or fix an issue, and never shares it with third parties for their own use.

5. Where it is stored & security

Data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) with access controls and security rules that enforce the separation described above. Connections are encrypted (HTTPS).

6. Children's information

Student records are provided by the school in its role as the educational institution. We process them only to deliver the service to that school and its authorised parents and staff.

7. Messages to parents

Absence alerts, fee reminders, and report cards are sent by the school's own staff through their own WhatsApp/SMS. {{ appName }} only helps prepare these messages.

8. Data retention & deletion

A school may request export or deletion of its data at any time. On ending the service, a school's data is removed within a reasonable period.

9. Contact

For any privacy question or request, contact us at {{ supportEmail }} or {{ supportPhone }}.

We may update this policy as the app evolves; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
